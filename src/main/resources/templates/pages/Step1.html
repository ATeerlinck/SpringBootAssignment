Step 1: Get what you need

To eat cereal, you need 4 things: the cereal of your choice, a bowl, a spoon, and milk, though milk is optional. You should get all of these out and set them on a countertop or a table, making sure you have enough room to pour the milk if you are going to use it.

In this example, we will be eating life cereal without milk. We need the box of cereal (though yours might come in a bag), a bowl, and a spoon.
