Step 3: Eating the cereal

Take your spoon, and scoop some cereal with the spoon. You should then put the spoon with the cereal on it into your mouth. You can close your mouth and pull the spoon out, taking the cereal off the spoon. Chew the cereal and swallow. Repeat until your bowl has no cereal in it. If you have milk in your bowl, you can drink the milk by putting the bowl up your mouth and drinking from the bowl as if it was a cup.
